Rota Conversion

  1. First, get your rota image and go to the Conversions page.
  2. Upload your rota image, enter the required details, and submit.
  3. You’ll then be redirected to the extracted data page.
  4. Click on a cell to enter the data to the form.
  5. Once the data is filled in, you can export it as a calendar file or add it to your Google Calendar.
  6. If the save to calendar setting is enabled, the shift will appear on the website’s calendar page.
